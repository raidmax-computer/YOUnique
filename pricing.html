Photoshoot Costs


All photoshoots cost R1500.00 for a 1 hour duration. Expect 60 postcard size digital photos. However you can expect about 50 more as all photos are edited and styled differently. Extend the shoot by 1 hour for double the photos for only R 600 more . I accommodate a maximum of 5 persons on a shoot location. If more persons are involved a surcharge of R 50 per extra person will be levied. No other cameras on site please.


If engaging my services to cover a function involving many persons and children the cost is R2500 for a period of 3 hours . Please refer to our FQA (frequently asked questions) section to find out more. We also have a photo booth that we can place on location . Contact me for more information.
