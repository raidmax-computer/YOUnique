Frequently Asked Questions

QUESTIONS

1. Do you use one photohrapher only?


2. Why do you not allow another camera on site?


3. Do you have a photo booth available for use in weddings, parties or other large functions?



4. Do your prices allow for any location?



5. Will you give us uneditted photos?


6. Will you provide us hard copy prints?


7. Can you provide a printed photo albumn?



8. Can you create a video slide show of our photos?

ANSWERS

1. Mostly yes, but depending on the type of shoot we can have an extra photographer for an additional cost of R500.00. You'd get double the photos.

2. Because it slows down the photography shoot when I need to wait for another person to take his/her photograph.

3. Yes, we do, with suitable backdrops. Usually offered by the host as a favor for guests. The booth is available for an additional cost of R 2500.00. Printing is done immediately (power point needed). Alernatively a guest charge of R50.00 per photo can be levied on site. Also, an extra photographer would be mandatory. Contact me for a quotation.

4. The prices are based on locations situated within a 30 km radius of our offices. A surcharge based on AA rates will apply for more distant locations. Contact me for a quotation.

5. No, we edit the photos to ensure the best results ourselves.


6. The prices DO NOT include hard copy prints. However we can arrange for selected photographs to be printed for you at an additional cost. Contact me for a quotation.

7. This can be done but will attract an additional cost that will depend on the number of pages, size and finish. Contact me for a quotation after the shoot.


8. Yes, for an additional cost of R 1000.00. We can add your choice of captions and accompanying music. The final video will be in an MPEG 4 format and loaded on a memory stick that must be provided by yourself. Contact me for details
